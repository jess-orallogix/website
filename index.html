Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

What exactly is OrallogIX?

OrallogIX produces standardized, food-grade allergen extracts derived from whole food sources. Each extract is a characterized protein preparation with a known composition and concentration, produced under controlled conditions and verified by third-party laboratories.

How is your product characterized?

Every production lot undergoes SDS-PAGE protein profiling to confirm the expected protein banding pattern. We provide a full Certificate of Analysis (CoA) with each order, and lots are independently validated by accredited third-party labs including InBio and Eurofins for identity and protein content.

What concentration are the extracts?

Our extracts are standardized to 10 mg/mL total protein. This is approximately double the concentration offered by most alternatives currently on the market, providing greater flexibility in how the product is used.

Which allergens are available?

Peanut extract is our first product and will be available beginning in mid-2026. Milk, egg, and tree nut extracts are on our development roadmap and will follow in subsequent phases.

Who typically purchases OrallogIX products?

Our products are purchased by licensed healthcare professionals — primarily allergists and allergy practices — as well as academic and clinical research laboratories. All orders are fulfilled directly to qualified purchasers.

Is OrallogIX an FDA-approved drug or biologic?

No. OrallogIX products are food-grade allergen extracts marketed as dietary supplement ingredients. They are not drugs, biologics, or investigational products, and they are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
